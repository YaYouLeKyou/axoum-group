History




@xoum Group is a communication agency that is specialized in providing various types of communication services. Axoum, also spelled Aksum, is an ancient city located in the northern region of Ethiopia. It was the capital of the Axumite Kingdom, which emerged around the 1st century AD and played an important role in the development of trade and culture in the region. Axum is famous for its towering obelisks, impressive ruins, and religious significance. It is believed to be the site of the Queen of Sheba's palace and a place of pilgrimage for Ethiopian Orthodox Christians. Axum was designated a UNESCO World Heritage site in 1980.
